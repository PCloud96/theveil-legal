Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

Cloud Motion Lab ("we", "us", "our") operates iOS applications including The Veil, Spirit Box Ghost Detector EVP, and other apps available on the Apple App Store. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Our apps run entirely on your device. We do not have user accounts, servers, or databases that store your data.

Microphone Access

Some of our apps (such as The Veil) request microphone access for interactive features. Audio captured through the microphone is processed locally on your device in real time and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted. We do not perform speech recognition or transcription. Microphone access is optional and the app provides alternative input methods if permission is denied.

Camera and Photo Access

Some of our apps may request camera or photo library access for specific features. Images are processed locally on your device and are never uploaded to external servers.

Analytics

We use TelemetryDeck for privacy-friendly analytics. TelemetryDeck does not collect personal data, does not use device fingerprinting, and does not track users across apps. Analytics data is aggregated and anonymized. No IP addresses are stored. For more information, visit TelemetryDeck's Privacy Policy.

Subscriptions and Purchases

In-app purchases and subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information, Apple ID, or billing details. Subscription management is handled through your Apple ID settings.

Advertising

Some of our apps display ads through Unity Ads. Unity may collect device-level data as described in their privacy policy. You can limit ad tracking through your device's privacy settings (Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking). If you opt out of tracking via Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt, personalized ads will not be shown.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it.

Data Storage

App preferences and settings are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms. This data stays on your device and is not synced to any external server.

Third-Party Services

Our apps may use the following third-party services:

  • Apple StoreKit (in-app purchases)
  • RevenueCat (subscription management)
  • TelemetryDeck (privacy-friendly analytics)
  • Unity Ads (advertising, in some apps)

Each service has its own privacy policy governing data handling.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
